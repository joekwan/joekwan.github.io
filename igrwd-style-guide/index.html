InvestorsGroup.com style guide

This guide is intended to aid in keeping our brand and website visually consistent as it grows and progresses. The more consistent and calculated we can be, the more professional and familier our brand will be. Drupalize.Me is a growing entity and therefore a brandguide is crucial in keeping our product on the right track. If there is anything unclear in this guide please bring it to the teams attention.
